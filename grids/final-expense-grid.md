# Final Expense Insurance Underwriting Grid

> **MASTER UNDERWRITING CONDITIONS TABLE** — Guides agent placement decisions for 5 major final expense carriers.

## Conditions Legend

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **DECLINE** | Not eligible — automatic decline |
| **Guaranteed Issue (GI)** | No health questions required, automatic approval |
| **Graded** | 2-year graded benefit (year 1: 30-40%, year 2: 70-80%, year 3+: 100%) |
| **Modified** | Similar to graded, limited benefit first 2 years |
| **ROP** | Return of Premium (if death in first 2 years, premiums returned) |
| **Standard** | Approved with standard rates |
| **Preferred** | Approved with best/preferred rates |
| **Basic** | Lowest tier of coverage offered by carrier |
| **Not asked - Allowed** | Carrier doesn't ask about this condition, so it's auto-allowed |

## Key Insights

- **Accendo (Aetna)**: Most flexible with Guaranteed Issue for serious conditions; best for complex health histories
- **New Vista**: Moderate rates, Standard to Preferred classes; middle-ground option
- **Senior Choice**: Graded benefits common; good for applicants with recent medical events (1-3 years)
- **Plan Right**: ROP focus; appeals to applicants concerned about premium return
- **Living Promise**: Most restrictive; typically requires longer recovery periods, declines more conditions
