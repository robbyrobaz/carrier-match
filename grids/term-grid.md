## Key Notes

- **Home Mortgage Series**: Most restrictive, many auto-declines
- **TruStage Term**: Many "Ineligible" routes to Ethos products instead
- **Family Freedom Term**: Moderate, many "see meds" conditions
- **TLE/IULE**: Generally stable, fewer conditions asked
- **Term Made Simple**: Also stable with reasonable guidelines

## Premium Tier Summary

- **Ethos Prime**: Best risk ($2M max)
- **Ethos Spectrum**: Moderate risk ($500K max)
- **Ethos Select**: Highest risk ($150K max)
