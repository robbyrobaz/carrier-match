## Products Overview

### Eagle Select 1 (Level Benefit)

**Issue Ages**: 40-85 (Non-nicotine); 40-85 (Nicotine)
**Face Amounts**: $5,000 - $50,000 (ages 40-75); $40,000 max (ages 76-85)
**Death Benefits**: Level for life of policy
**Underwriting Classes**: Nonsmoker/Smoker; Male/Female
**Premium Structure**: Annual policy fee of $40 (commissionable)

**Key Features**:
- Instant decision eApplication
- Simple application process
- Quit Smoking Advantage — smokers qualify for nonsmoker rates after quit
- Policy eDelivery
- Accelerated Benefit Payment Rider (included at no additional cost)

**Optional Riders**:
- Accidental Death Benefit Rider (Series 2172/2313)
- Child and Grandchild Term Rider (Series 2194)

**Modal Factors**:
- Annual: 1.00
- Monthly PAC: 0.095

**State Availability**: All 50 states + DC (Series 311/312)
