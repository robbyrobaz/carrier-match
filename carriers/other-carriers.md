# Other Carriers — Quick Reference

> Reference information for additional final expense and whole life carriers mentioned in agent networks but not covered in detailed source documents.

## American Amicable

**Headquarters**: Waco, TX
**Specialty**: Final Expense / Burial Insurance
**Issue Ages**: 50-85
**Face Amounts**: $1,000 - $50,000
**Underwriting**: Simplified issue, graded benefit plans available

**Key Products**:
- Whole Life (ages 50-85)
- Graded Whole Life (2-3 year graded period)
- Accidental Death Benefit Rider

**Strengths**:
- Very liberal underwriting
- Accepts applicants declined by others
- Graded plans for health challenges
- Straightforward application process

**Website**: americanamicable.com

---

## Corebridge Financial (formerly AIG Life)

**Headquarters**: New York, NY (AIG successor)
**Specialty**: Permanent Life Insurance
**Issue Ages**: 18-85
**Face Amounts**: Varies by product ($5,000 - $2,000,000+)
**Underwriting**: Full underwriting, simplified express options available

**Key Products**:
- Term Life
- Whole Life (Participating and Non-participating)
- IUL (Indexed Universal Life)
- VUL (Variable Universal Life)

**Strengths**:
- Strong financial ratings
- Wide product range
- Good for diverse underwriting scenarios
- Participating whole life available (dividend option)

**Website**: corebridgefinancial.com

## Foresters Financial

**Headquarters**: Toronto, Canada (North American operations)
**Specialty**: Term and Permanent Life Insurance
**Issue Ages**: 18-80 (varies by product)
**Face Amounts**: $50,000 - $1,000,000+
**Underwriting**: Standard to simplified

**Key Products**:
- Term Life (10, 20, 30 year options)
- Whole Life
- Universal Life
- Final Expense Plans

**Strengths**:
- Fraternal organization (credit union partnerships)
- Competitive rates
- Good underwriting for standard risks
- Canada-based stability

**Website**: forestersfin.com

## Carrier Selection Guide

| Applicant Profile | Best Carrier |
|-------------------|--------------|
| **Excellent health, any age** | National Life, Corebridge, Ethos Prime |
| **Fair health, 50-85** | American Amicable, Foresters, Accendo |
| **Complex medical history** | American Amicable, Ethos Select, Accendo |
| **Fast underwriting needed** | Ethos, Instabrain (if active), Transamerica |
| **Ages 18-40, standard risk** | National Life, Term carriers, Corebridge |
| **Ages 50-85, final expense** | American Amicable, Americo, Senior Choice |
| **Graded benefit needed** | American Amicable, Senior Choice, Ethos Final Expense |
| **Large face amount ($500K+)** | National Life, Corebridge, Ethos Prime |
| **Simplified underwriting** | Transamerica, Ethos, Foresters Express |

## Notes for Agents

1. **Always Verify Current Status**: Carrier products, underwriting guidelines, and state availability change frequently
2. **Check State Licensing**: Ensure carrier licensed in applicant's state before submission
3. **Compare Turnaround Times**: Ranges from instant (digital) to 15 days (traditional)
4. **Understand Rate Classes**: Same condition may result in different ratings/tiers across carriers
5. **Know Your Benchmarks**: Build networks with top carriers for your geographic area and applicant profile
