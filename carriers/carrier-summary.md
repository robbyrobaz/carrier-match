## Carrier Underwriting Philosophy Comparison

### Most Flexible (Liberal) — Accept Complex Cases
- **Accendo**: Guaranteed Issue for many severe conditions
- **American Amicable**: Most liberal final expense carrier
- **Ethos Final Expense**: Simplified approach, flexible declines
- **Senior Choice**: Good for graded/recent events

### Moderate — Standard Risk Focus
- **Mutual of Omaha**: Flexible with mild-moderate conditions
- **New Vista**: Middle-ground underwriting
- **TruStage**: Simplified but still selective

### Fastest — Instant Decisioning
- **Transamerica**: <10 minutes typical
- **Americo**: Instant eApp decisions
- **Ethos Products**: Instant to same-day

### Most Restrictive — Best Health Only
- **National Life**: Standard full underwriting
- **Corebridge**: Traditional UW approach
- **Home Mortgage Series**: Strict mortgage criteria

## Age-Based Carrier Recommendations

### Ages 18-40 (Young Adults)
**Best Choices**: Ethos Prime, National Life (Term), Family Freedom Term
**Avoid**: Senior Choice, American Amicable (age restrictions)

### Ages 40-50 (Middle-Aged)
**Best Choices**: Americo Eagle Select, Accendo, Ethos Spectrum, Transamerica
**Good Backup**: Mutual of Omaha, New Vista

### Ages 50-70 (Pre-Retirement)
**Best Choices**: Senior Choice, Accendo, Americo, Transamerica, Ethos Final Expense
**Flexible**: American Amicable, Mutual of Omaha

### Ages 70-85 (Retirement)
**Best Choices**: American Amicable, Accendo, Transamerica, Senior Choice
**Face Amount Caps**: Note age 76+ reductions (most carriers $25K max vs $100K at younger ages)

## Condition-Specific Carrier Selection

### Recent Heart Attack/Surgery (Within 2 Years)
1. **Accendo** — Guaranteed Issue option
2. **Transamerica** — Fast underwriting
3. **Senior Choice** — Graded benefit available
4. **Avoid**: National Life, most term carriers

### Advanced Cancer / Terminal Diagnosis
1. **Accendo** — Flexible with GI options
2. **American Amicable** — Most liberal
3. **Ethos Final Expense** — Simplified UW
4. **Avoid**: National Life, term carriers, very restrictive

### Dialysis / End-Stage Renal Disease
1. **Accendo** — Kidney Dialysis - GI
2. **Senior Choice** — May decline or modify
3. **Ethos Final Expense** — Case by case
4. **Avoid**: Most strict underwriters

### Diabetes (Controlled)
1. **Mutual of Omaha** — Flexible if controlled
2. **Senior Choice** — Acceptable
3. **Accendo** — GI for complications
4. **Ethos** — Spectrum or Select tier

### Recent Stroke (Within 1-2 Years)
1. **Accendo** — <1yr GI option
2. **Transamerica** — Fast decisioning
3. **Senior Choice** — Graded benefit
4. **Avoid**: National Life, term carriers

### Multiple Chronic Conditions
1. **American Amicable** — Most liberal
2. **Accendo** — GI for severe
3. **Ethos Final Expense** — Simplified
4. **Senior Choice** — Standard approach

### Mental Health / Behavioral Issues
1. **Ethos** — Product routing available
2. **Accendo** — Generally "Not asked"
3. **Mutual of Omaha** — Flexible if stable
4. **Avoid**: National Life (strict criteria)
