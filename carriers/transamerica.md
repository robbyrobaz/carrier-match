## Product Overview

### Transamerica FE Express Solution™ (Immediate Benefit)

**Product Type**: Nonparticipating whole life with instant decisioning
**Issue Ages**: 18-85
**Face Amounts**:
- Ages 18-75: Up to $100,000
- Ages 76-85: Up to $25,000
- Minimum: $5,000

**Death Benefit**: Guaranteed level benefit (immediate full coverage)
**Premium Structure**: Level premiums to age 100

**Risk Classes**:
- Select Nontobacco
- Select Tobacco

**Ownership**: Insured same as owner
**Age Basis**: Age last birthday

**Turnaround Time**: Instant decisions in as few as 10 minutes

---

### Transamerica Graded FE Express Solution™ (Graded Benefit)

**Product Type**: Nonparticipating whole life with graded benefit
**Issue Ages**: 18-80
**Face Amounts**:
- Minimum: $5,000
- Maximum: $25,000
- Product matures at age 121

**Premium Structure**: Level premiums to age 121

**Risk Classes**:
- Nontobacco
- Tobacco

**Ownership**: Insured same as owner
**Age Basis**: Age last birthday
